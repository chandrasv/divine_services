About Saravana

Four Decades of Devoted Service

Saravana is a dedicated temple priest with around 40 years of experience performing sacred rituals and guiding families through traditional ceremonies. His journey began in the ancient temples of South India, where he trained in the rigorous Vedic traditions.

For the past several years, he has been serving the Hindu community in Singapore, offering the same level of devotion and authenticity that he practiced in India. He specializes in making complex rituals accessible while maintaining their spiritual integrity.

Whether you need a simple archana at the temple or an elaborate homam at your new home, Saravana brings patience, clarity, and genuine care to every ceremony.
