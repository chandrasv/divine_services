About Saravana

Trusted Hindu Priest With 40+ Years of Experience

Saravana is a dedicated Hindu temple priest with around 40 years of experience performing Vedic rituals and guiding families through traditional samskaras. His journey began in the ancient temples of South India, where he trained in rigorous Vedic discipline.

For the past several years, he has served the Hindu community in Singapore, offering authentic puja, homam, and pooja procedures with the same devotion practiced in India. He makes complex rituals accessible while preserving spiritual integrity.

Whether you need a simple archana at the temple, a house warming ceremony, or an elaborate homam at your new home, Saravana brings patience, clarity, and genuine care to every ceremony.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking Hindu divine services in Singapore.

Which divine services are available?

Saravana offers House Warming (Griha Pravesh), Satyanarayana Puja, Homam/Havan, Archana, Naming and Annaprashana ceremonies, Muhurtham guidance, Ganapathy Homam, Sudharshanam Homam, Mahamruthyunjaya Homam, Aayushya Homam, Mahalakshmi Homam, Navagraha Homam, Jataga Parihara Homam, Kala Sarpa Santhi, 60th/70th/80th ceremonies, Punnya Vachanam, and guidance on good days for engagement, marriage, and other occasions.

Can ceremonies be performed at home?

Yes. Most services can be performed at your home anywhere in Singapore. Temple and online guidance options are also available where suitable.

What languages are supported?

Ceremonies and guidance are available in English, Tamil, and Telugu, with clear explanations for families and elders.

How early should I book?

For better availability, book one to two weeks in advance. For weekends, festival dates, or complex homam ceremonies, earlier booking is recommended.
